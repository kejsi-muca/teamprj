FENCING

This project was done for a client who wanted to promote their work and the passion for the sport that their group has been doing.There are some types of Fencing. The first page there is a brief explanation of these sports. The websites idea is to inform people about this sport but also inspire and motivate them to start doing it too. The website also provides a slideshow of fencing “heroes”, champions who had won main championships.The webpage also provides a historical book of events, where people can see and picture themselves in a certain situation.

SKIING WONDERLAND

This is an amazing project we had to do for an amazing group of people.It is an informative website where people can go to check the latest news, weather, snow conditions, hotels and their prices. It also describes some benefits of skiing and presents some of the best views of Kodra e Diellit-Popova Sapka. In the information part there is a slideshow of pictures of hotels that are in this wonderful place.There is also a what to do while there part where you can view all the wonderful things and opportunities that this ski resort is offering for good prices.
